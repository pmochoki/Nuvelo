# Policies

## Privacy Policy (draft points)
- Collects basic account info (name, email/phone).
- Stores listing content, images, and messages.
- Uses data for service delivery and safety enforcement.
- Users can request deletion of account and data.

## Terms of Service (draft points)
- Users are responsible for listing accuracy.
- Prohibited content: scams, illegal goods, harassment.
- Reports are reviewed and content may be removed.
- Accounts may be suspended for violations.

## Content Guidelines
- No illegal items or services.
- No hate speech or discrimination.
- No misleading or fraudulent listings.
