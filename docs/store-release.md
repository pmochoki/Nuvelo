# Store Release Checklist

## App Store (iOS)
- App icons (1024x1024) and screenshots
- Privacy policy and terms hosted online
- Contact email and support URL

## Google Play
- App icons, feature graphic, screenshots
- Privacy policy link
- Content rating questionnaire

## Huawei AppGallery
- App icon, screenshots, app description
- Privacy policy link
- Content rating (if required)

## Beta plan
- Internal testing with 10-20 users
- Closed beta with 100-200 users
- Track metrics: daily active users, listing creation, chat initiation, report rate
