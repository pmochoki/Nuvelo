InterHungary Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-26

InterHungary is a community marketplace where users can browse listings, post items, and chat securely. This policy explains how we collect and use information.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, email, or phone number.
  • Listings you create, including photos and descriptions.
  • Messages you send through in-app chat.
  • Basic device information to diagnose issues.

How we use information

  • Provide and improve marketplace features.
  • Enable secure chat between buyers and sellers.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Maintain safety, prevent abuse, and moderate content.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information only when required by law or to protect users and the service.

Data retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion by contacting support.

Your choices

  • Update or correct your account information.
  • Request account deletion and data removal.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at pmochoki@gmail.com.
