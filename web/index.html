How to Buy Safely on Nuvelo

Most transactions on Nuvelo are smooth and honest. These steps keep you protected every time.

🔍 Before you contact a seller

Read the full listing: description, condition, and photos.

Check the seller's profile — look for the ✅ Verified badge, feedback score, and how long they've been active.

If the price seems too good to be true, treat it as a red flag.

Use the location filter to find sellers near you.

💬 Communicating with the seller

Keep all conversations on Nuvelo so there's a record.

Ask about the exact condition, defects, receipt availability, and preferred meeting spot.

Never share your home address, bank details, or personal documents before meeting in person.

Be cautious if a seller pushes you off-platform to WhatsApp, Telegram, or email before the deal is confirmed.

📍 Arranging the meetup

Always meet in a busy public place during daylight — a shopping centre, café, or near a police station.

Bring a friend for high-value purchases.

Test electronics thoroughly before paying. Ask for serial numbers.

For rental viewings, never hand over a deposit before signing a written contract.

💳 Making payment

Pay in person — cash or traceable bank transfer only.

Never pay via gift cards, crypto, or irreversible wire transfers.

Never send money before seeing and testing the item.

For high-value deals, use a written receipt with both names, item description, price, date, and signatures.

✅ After the transaction

Leave feedback on the seller's profile.

If something went wrong, use the "Report" button on their profile.

Email support@nuvelo.one with screenshots if you suspect fraud.

✅ Verified Sellers on Nuvelo

When you see the ✅ Verified badge on a listing or seller profile, it means that seller has passed Nuvelo's identity and trust check.

🔎 What does Verified mean?

A seller earns the ✅ badge by completing one or more of these steps:

Confirming identity with a government-issued ID

Verifying their phone number via SMS

Verifying their email address

Building a positive feedback history with no unresolved disputes

(Businesses) Providing a company registration number

The badge is manually reviewed by the Nuvelo team — it is never granted automatically.

🛡️ Why buy from Verified sellers?

Lower fraud risk — real identity on file makes anonymous scams much harder.

Accountability — if a dispute arises, we have verified contact details to assist resolution.

Better listing quality — Verified sellers are held to stricter content standards.

Faster support — reports against Verified sellers are reviewed with priority.

Filterable — use the "Verified sellers" filter in Browse to see only verified listings.

⚠️ Does Verified = guaranteed?

No. The badge confirms identity and reduces risk — it does not guarantee item quality or transaction outcome. Always inspect before paying and follow safe buying practices.

🏷️ How to become Verified

Go to your Profile → Settings

Find the "Get Verified" section

Submit your verification documents

Expect a review within 1–2 business days

Verification is free during the current MVP period.

🚩 Spotted a fake Verified badge?

Report the listing immediately. Misuse of the badge violates our Terms & Conditions and results in account suspension.
